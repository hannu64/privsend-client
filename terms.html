What privsend is

privsend lets you send a secret that is encrypted in your browser and destroyed the first time it is read. A secret can be a message, files, or both — up to 10 files, 25 MB in total. We only ever receive ciphertext. We cannot read your secrets, and we cannot recover them. The decryption key never reaches us: it travels only in the part of the link after the “#”, which browsers do not send to servers.

privsend is not storage and not a backup. Every secret is destroyed when it is read, or when it expires — whichever comes first. Attached files are destroyed within 60 minutes of the read; our privacy notice explains why that short window exists. Never make privsend the only copy of anything.

privsend does not deliver anything

We ask for no names, no phone numbers and no email addresses — not from senders, and not from recipients. There are no accounts.

We also do not send anything to anyone. We hand the link to you, and you pass it on through a channel of your own choosing — email, chat, phone, in person. We never learn who the recipient is and we hold no address of any kind for anybody. privsend is not a messaging service. It stores an encrypted secret until whoever holds the link opens it.

Reporting a link, and takedown

Because content is encrypted before it reaches us, we cannot inspect it, and we cannot moderate proactively. We can only act on the link.

If you believe a link is being misused, report it. You do not need to prove anything, and reporting a link does not disclose its contents to us — your browser strips the decryption key before the report is sent. We review reports and destroy links that breach these terms. You may also write to support@zumitomi.fi.

If your secret is removed

If we remove a secret you created, your status link will say so. Because we hold no account and no address for you, that receipt is the only way we can reach you — so we use it, rather than letting the link quietly go dead. If you believe a removal was a mistake, contact support@zumitomi.fi and we will look at it again.
